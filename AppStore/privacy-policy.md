# ForzAdvisor Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-31

Public URL: https://blackslabpro.github.io/ForzAdvisor/privacy/

ForzAdvisor is an unofficial racing-game tuning assistant. This policy explains how the app handles information in version 1.0.

## Information Processed On Device

ForzAdvisor can process car details, tune settings, player notes, photos, imported screenshots, and saved tune history. By default, this information stays on your device and is used to generate, display, save, search, and adjust tunes.

Photos and imported screenshots are processed on device with Apple's Vision OCR. The app may save a small local thumbnail with a saved tune if you choose to save that tune.

## Optional API Mode

ForzAdvisor runs offline by default. If you choose API mode and save your own Anthropic API key, the app sends confirmed car details, selected discipline, and any relevant player notes to Anthropic to generate or adjust a tune. Screenshots are not uploaded by ForzAdvisor in version 1.0.

Your Anthropic API key is stored in the iOS Keychain on your device. It is sent to Anthropic only as part of API requests you initiate through API mode.

## Tracking And Advertising

ForzAdvisor does not include advertising SDKs, does not sell personal information, and does not track you across apps or websites.

## Data Controls

You can delete saved tunes in the app. You can clear the optional Anthropic API key in Settings. You can disable camera access in iOS Settings.

## Children

ForzAdvisor is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the public support tracker at https://github.com/blackslabpro/ForzAdvisor/issues.
